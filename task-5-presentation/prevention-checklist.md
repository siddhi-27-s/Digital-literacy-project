# **Stay Safe Onilne**

* Avoid scanning random QR codes shared on social media or chats   
* Avoid using Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive logins  
* If you've been scammed immediately report to [cybercrime.gov.in](http://cybercrime.gov.in) and call **Helpline: 1930** for urgent financial fraud assistance  
* Never share OTPs, PINs or passswords with anyone  
* Always Remember, you only enter your pin to send money not to recieve it.  
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) everywhere  
* Identify and avoid phishing links sent via SMS or WhatsApp  
* Think before you share your digital footprint is permanent  
* Use strong and unique passwors for every account
