# RESPONSIBLE SOCIAL MEDIA USE AS A COLLEGE STUDENT

## Do’s

- [ ] Give credit when posting others’ content  
- [ ] Set daily screen time limits  
- [ ] Report and block harassers  
- [ ] Maintain a positive and professional online presence  
- [ ] Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts  
      

## Don’ts

- [ ] Don’t spread unverified news  
- [ ] Don’t share your location publicly  
- [ ] Don’t compare yourself to online influencers  
- [ ] Don’t post offensive or hateful content  
- [ ] Don’t leak official documents
